About Nike Collection

Innovation in Every Step

At Nike Collection, we're more than just a shoe company. We're innovators, designers, and dreamers. Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. If you have a body, you are an athlete.

We believe in the power of sport to move the world forward. From our sustainable practices to our community initiatives, we're committed to creating a better future for all.

The Nike Journey

1964

Blue Ribbon Sports founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight.

1971

Nike's iconic Swoosh logo is created by Carolyn Davidson.

1984

Nike signs Michael Jordan and launches Air Jordan brand.

1988

"Just Do It" slogan is introduced and becomes a global phenomenon.

Present

Nike continues to innovate and inspire athletes worldwide.
